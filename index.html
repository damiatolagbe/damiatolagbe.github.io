Let Us Stop Deforestation

Overview

Deforestation is the purposeful clearing of forested land. The loss of trees and other vegetation can cause climate change, desertification, soil erosion, fewer crops, flooding, increased greenhouse gases in the atmosphere, and a host of problems for people.
